CineTweet is an iPad app that lets you display your tweets for presentation purposes such as trade shows, corporate events, and music festivals. You can change background colors and font color to match your branding style guides, and even upload a logo or background image.


  • The "HELP" button brings up a list of frequently asked questions and topics.
  • The "SETTINGS" button opens a dialog box that allows you to save your current configurations.
  • The "HIDE" button minimizes the configuration interface and hides the top navigation.
  • Change your search parameters by typing a keyword into the “search” text field and clicking “GO!” CineTweet will search Twitter for all tweets containing your keyword.

    If "Tweets" display counter reads zero, no tweets are found and you will need to change your search terms and try again.
  • To change color:
    Use the "color wheel" to change your background and font colors-- click "Font Color" to change font color styles, and click "Background Color" to change the color of CineTweet's background.
  • To upload a background image or logo:
    Click the "upload logo" button and browse the Photo Gallery for your iDevice. CineTweet will automatically position the image in the center of the display.

    The recommended image dimensons are 1024px wide and 728px high, saved as a .PNG, .JPG, or .GIF. Taking a picture with your mobile device may produce an image that is too big or the wrong format for CineTweet to import correctly. In addition, if your image has a transparent background (such as a .GIF or a .PNG) it may not display correctly due to the way iOS re-compresses images on import.
  • To change animation speed:
    The "Speed" slider changes the rate of the animation. It affects both the transition speed between the tweets, as well as the length of time the tweet is visible.
  • To change animation type:
    The "Animation Type" button allows you to change the way CineTweet transitions between tweets-- currently it supports fade-in or scrolling display.
  • To show or hide the profile picture:
    The "Show Pic" toggle will change the visibility of the user's profile picture.
